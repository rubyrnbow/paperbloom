How EcoPot Works

Step 1: Collect used paper waste

We gather discarded paper materials to give them a new purpose.

Step 2: Soak in Water

The paper that been shredded will be soak in a water and we let it sit until it soften.

Step 3: Blend the Paper

The wet paper will be blend in the chopper to make it even more fine.

Step 4: Mix The Paper

The paper will be mixed with few ingredients that would make the paper more durable for planting purpose.

Step 4: Mold The Mixture

The mixture is shaped into pots and dried until sturdy and ready for planting .

Step 5: Plant & Let Nature Take Over

Place soil and seeds in your EcoPot, then watch it enrich the earth as it biodegrades.
